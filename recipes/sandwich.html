Description

This is the best recipe you will find for making grilled cheese sandwiches. Read on for the full recipe!

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar
  • Pepper Jack
  • Butter

    • Steps

  • Butter both side of the bread
  • Place slice down in hot nonstick pan
  • mix cream cheese with shredded cheeses
  • spread cheese mixture on bread
  • Top one slice on the other
  • Serve!
