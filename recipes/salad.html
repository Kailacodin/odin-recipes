Description

This recipe will show you how to make a simple summer salad to accompany your meals.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Red onion
  • Apples
  • Olive oil
  • Vineagar
  • Salt and Pepper

    • Steps

  • Cut up Onion
  • Cut up apples
  • Toss together apples, greens, onion, oil, Vineagar
  • Top with salt and pepper to taste
